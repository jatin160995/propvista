Citizenship by Investment

The Process

How It Works

A clear, transparent 3-step process handled entirely by our expert team.

1

Choose Your Property

Browse our citizenship-eligible listings and select a property that meets the minimum $400,000 investment threshold. We'll help you evaluate ROI and location.

2

Complete the Purchase

Our legal team handles the full conveyancing process — title deed verification, tax registration, and official property transfer — with complete transparency.

3

Apply for Citizenship

We submit your citizenship application to Turkish authorities, manage all documentation, and guide you through biometrics. Average approval: 90 days.

About Us

A Decade of Trusted Excellence

PropVista was founded in Dubai with a mission to make premium international real estate accessible to global investors.

Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Results

Founded in 2014, PropVista emerged from a simple observation: high-net-worth investors worldwide were underserved when it came to international real estate. Complex regulations, language barriers, and lack of transparency created friction that prevented smart investment decisions.

We built PropVista to change that. Starting with a small team of 5 in Dubai, we've grown to 60+ professionals across offices in Dubai and Istanbul — serving over 2,000 clients from 20+ countries.

Our specialization in citizenship by investment programs, combined with deep local market knowledge, has made us the go-to partner for investors who want to secure not just property, but their family's future.
